# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, engineering recruiters, potential freelance clients, and fellow developers evaluating Ahmed Lotfy's skills and project experience. They visit to assess technical competence, quality of work, and design sensibility. Context: desktop browsing during work hours, mobile browsing during commutes. Primary emotion: confidence in the developer's ability.

## Product Purpose

A bilingual (EN/AR) portfolio that demonstrates senior full-stack engineering capability through both the work shown AND the quality of the portfolio itself. Every pixel proves the developer's craft. Success = the visitor reaches out for a consultation, job opportunity, or collaboration.

## Brand Personality

Precise · Confident · Premium

Tone is direct and technical — no fluff, no hype. Speaks with the authority of someone who has shipped production systems. Voice balances professional rigor with approachability.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-designed portfolios (purple gradients, mesh backgrounds, overused glassmorphism)
- Boring developer portfolios (default Tailwind, stock templates, no personality)
- Overly flashy "creative agency" style that undermines technical credibility
- SaaS clichés (hero-metric template, feature card grids, "Boost / Elevate / Unleash" copy)

## Design Principles

1. **Portfolio as proof** — The design of the portfolio itself is the first and most important project shown. Every interaction, every spacing decision, every transition communicates the developer's skill.
2. **Technical confidence** — Restrained, precise, not shouting. Hierarchy through weight and space, not decoration. Dark cinematic background with a single blue accent — controlled, not gaudy.
3. **Bilingual parity** — English and Arabic experiences are equally polished. RTL layout is not an afterthought.
4. **Performance is design** — 95+ Lighthouse scores are a feature. The portfolio must load fast, animate smoothly, and respect reduced motion.
5. **Every pixel intentional** — No filler UI, no decorative elements without purpose. Copy is lean. Animations communicate hierarchy or delight, never just "because."

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respects prefers-reduced-motion. Supports system font scaling. Keyboard navigable. ARIA labels on all icon-only controls.
